Week 6 

>
 In week 6 of Digital Engineering, we learned how to use Corel Draw and the Roland
GS-24/540i to design and create vinyl stickers. We first created our design, then uploaded it to Corel Draw, then uploaded
that .eps file to the Roland machine and cut out our stickers. This week we also created a silicon mold by using subtractive
milling through Fusion 360. Lastly, we created a coaster by creating a urethane and epoxy mixture and molded it in a plastic container.
