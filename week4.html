Week 4 

>
 In week 4, we used the laser cutter to make a jigsaw puzzle, a fit kit, 
a coaster, and a box. To make these items, we first used Fusion 360 to make a
blueprint for the laser printer to know where to cut. Then we added a design that
the laser cutter carved into the wood/cork (for the box and coaster). For the puzzle,
we had to use the UV printer to print a picture on the cardboard before cutting it.
