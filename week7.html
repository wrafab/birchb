Week 7 

>
 In week 7 of Digital Engineering, we created a skateboard using vacuum
bagging and laser engraving. We first put a design on the skatboard by using either the laser cutter or vinyl
cutter (I used the laser cutter to engrave a design). After that we took the wood layers and sealed them in an
air-tight vacuum bag to form them to the premade mold. Lastly, sanded the sides of the skateboard and added
lamination to protect the surfaces.
